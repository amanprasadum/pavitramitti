Detailed Care Guide
Sunlight Requirement
  • Prefers bright indirect sunlight
  • Avoid direct harsh sunlight to prevent leaf damage
Watering
  • Water once every 7–10 days depending on climate
  • Keep soil slightly moist but well-drained
  • Avoid overwatering
Soil Type
  • Use well-drained, nutrient-rich soil mix
  • Ideal mix: Garden soil + compost + cocopeat
Fertilization
  • Feed once every 4–6 weeks with mild liquid fertilizer
  • Supports healthy leaf growth and color
Pruning
  • Remove yellow or damaged leaves regularly
  • Helps maintain plant shape and health
