Detailed Care Guide
Sunlight Requirement
  • Requires 4–6 hours of direct sunlight daily
  • Best suited for outdoor or bright indoor placement
Watering
  • Water 2–3 times per week depending on weather
  • Keep soil moist but well-drained
  • Avoid overwatering
Soil Type
  • Use well-drained, nutrient-rich soil mix
  • Ideal mix: Garden soil + compost + sand/cocopeat
Fertilization
  • Feed every 2–3 weeks with organic fertilizer
  • Helps in faster growth and more flowering
Pruning
  • Regular pruning promotes bushy growth and frequent blooms
  • Remove dry or damaged leaves and stems
