Frequently Asked Questions
Is Aloe Vera good for beginners?
Yes! Aloe Vera is one of the easiest plants to grow. It needs minimal watering and care, making it ideal for first-time plant parents.
Will I receive the plant in a pot?
The plant comes in a 4–6 inch nursery pot. A decorative pot, if shown in images, may not be included.
How often should I water this plant?
Water once every 7–10 days. Let the soil dry out completely between waterings to avoid root rot.
Is this plant pet friendly?
Aloe Vera can be mildly toxic to pets if ingested. Keep it out of reach of cats and dogs.
Does it need direct sunlight?
It prefers bright indirect light. It can tolerate a few hours of direct sun but avoid intense afternoon sun.
How big will the plant grow?
Aloe Vera can grow up to 1–2 feet tall indoors. With good light and occasional repotting, it grows steadily.
