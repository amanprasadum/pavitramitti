Detailed Care Guide
Sunlight Requirement
  • Needs 4–6 hours of direct sunlight daily
  • Best placed in balcony, terrace, or near a sunny window
Watering
  • Water 2–3 times per week depending on weather
  • Keep soil moist but well-drained
  • Avoid waterlogging
Soil Type
  • Use well-drained, fertile soil mix
  • Ideal mix: Garden soil + compost + sand/cocopeat
Fertilization
  • Feed every 2–3 weeks with organic fertilizer
  • Encourages more blooms and healthier growth
Pruning
  • Prune regularly for bushy growth and continuous flowering
  • Remove dried leaves and old stems
