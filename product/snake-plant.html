Detailed Care Guide
Sunlight Requirement
  • Grows well in low to bright indirect sunlight
  • Can tolerate partial shade indoors
Watering
  • Water once every 10–14 days depending on climate
  • Allow soil to dry completely between watering
  • Avoid overwatering
Soil Type
  • Use well-drained sandy or succulent soil mix
  • Ideal mix: Garden soil + sand + compost
Fertilization
  • Feed once every 4–6 weeks with mild fertilizer
  • Over-fertilizing is not necessary
Pruning
  • Remove dry or damaged leaves as needed
  • Helps maintain plant health and appearance
