Who We Are

The Heart Behind
Pavitra Mitti

Pavitra Mitti is a leading plant nursery located in Basirhat, near Barasat and Kolkata — recognized as one of the best nurseries in West Bengal. We provide a wide variety of plants nurtured with care and expertise, grown in healthy soil and tended by experienced hands.

"Pavitra Mitti" — meaning Sacred Soil — reflects our deepest belief: that healthy soil, honest care and genuine passion are the roots of everything beautiful that grows.

Whether you are a home gardener looking for your first plant, a landscaper needing bulk quantities, or a farmer expanding your orchard — our nursery offers everything you need to bring greenery and life to your space. We stock 200+ varieties across fruit plants, flowering plants, ornamental varieties, medicinal herbs and farm & landscape trees.

Serving the communities of Basirhat, Barasat, Kolkata and across West Bengal, we are proud to be the nursery that families return to — season after season.
